About Us

Studio Marani, a communication agency based in Milan, was founded in 2011 by Maurizio Marani following his extensive experience with the renowned McCann Erickson. Many of his esteemed clients, including L'Espresso Group and Radio Deejay, joined him in this new venture, along with new additions such as Radio Capital.

Our Mission

At Studio Marani, our mission is to help millions of organizations grow better. We believe in not just expanding in size, but in evolving and improving. Our commitment is to provide innovative communication strategies that foster sustainable growth and meaningful connections for our clients. We believe in creating win-win situations by aligning the success of our business with the success of our customers.

In 2004, fellow MIT graduate students Brian Halligan and Dharmesh Shah noticed a major shift in the way people shop and purchase products. Buyers didn't want to be interrupted by ads; they wanted helpful information. In 2006, they founded HubSpot to help companies use that shift to grow better with inbound marketing. Along the way, HubSpot expanded beyond marketing into a crafted, not cobbled suite of products that create the frictionless customer experience that buyers expect today. Expertly led by CEO Yamini Rangan, HubSpot uses its customer platform built on an AI-powered Smart CRM to help millions of scaling organizations grow better.
