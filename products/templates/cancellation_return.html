Cancellation & Returns

Customers can cancel their orders at any time before the product has been shipped. Once an order is canceled, a full refund will be processed, and the amount will be credited back to the original payment method within 5–7 business days. For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, the refund will be provided in the form of store credits or via bank transfer. In certain situations, the seller may need to cancel an order due to issues such as stock unavailability, pricing errors, or payment-related problems. If this occurs, the customer will be informed promptly through email or SMS, and any amount paid will be refunded in full. Please note that certain products labeled as “Non-cancellable” on their respective product pages cannot be canceled after the order is placed.

We want our customers to have a smooth and hassle-free shopping experience. Products can be returned within 7, 14, or 30 days (as specified for each product) from the date of delivery. To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, undamaged, and returned in its original packaging with all tags, accessories, and documentation intact. Certain products, such as innerwear, cosmetics, perishable goods, or customized items, are non-returnable unless there is a manufacturing defect or the item was damaged during delivery. In the event of a damaged or defective product, customers must report the issue within 48 hours of delivery and provide proof in the form of photos or videos. After verification, we will arrange for a replacement or issue a refund, depending on the customer’s preference. To initiate a return, customers can visit the “My Orders” section on their account and follow the return process. Depending on the policy, we will arrange for a pickup, or the customer may be required to ship the product back to us. If return shipping is required, the cost will be borne by the seller, provided the return is due to an error on our part (such as a defective or wrong item). Once the returned item is received and inspected, a refund will be processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days. For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds will be issued as store credits or via direct bank transfer.
